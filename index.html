Business Management softwares engineered for African SMEs.

Kwetu Partners Ltd is a Kenyan technology company specializing in the development and delivery of Software as a Service (SaaS) solutions for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across Africa. Based on our 25 years of experience in fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) industry and principles of Japanese efficiency, we create streamlined, industry-specific management software designed for rapid deployment. Our tools help businesses manage sales, inventory, operations, and more, with transparent pricing, no hidden fees, and founder-led onboarding.
