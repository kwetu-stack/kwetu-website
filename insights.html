Message from Our CEO

I’m Bundi Murithi, the CEO and founder of Kwetu Partners Ltd. With over 25 years of experience in the FMCG industry, I’ve lived through the daily struggles of sales teams and small businesses — taking orders in notebooks, sharing them through WhatsApp, stock variances, delivery inefficiencies, and the general disconnect between teams and operations.

When I shifted into software engineering, I made it my mission to build solutions inspired by real problems experienced in the field. My background gives me a unique advantage — I don’t just write code, I solve the pains I went through myself.

My vision is simple and focused: To help SMEs in Africa grow, digitize, and operate more efficiently using accessible management software.

Every tool we create at Kwetu Partners is purpose-driven — built to make business easier, faster, and smarter.
